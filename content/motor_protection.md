### Overview
Motor protection includes overload protection (thermal overloads) and short-circuit/ground-fault protection. Settings depend on motor FLA, service factor, and code requirements for the protection type.

### Typical equations
$$ I_{OL}=k\cdot I_{FLA}\quad\text{(overload setting)} $$
$$ I_{SC}=m\cdot I_{FLA}\quad\text{(short-circuit device sizing placeholder)} $$
