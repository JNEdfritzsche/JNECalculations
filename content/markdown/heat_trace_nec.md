## Core concepts

- Heat trace maintains or elevates temperature on pipes, vessels, or panels.
- Required heat output depends on ambient conditions, insulation, and allowable temperature drop.
- Electrical design is typically based on the heat trace cable watt rating (W/m) and total length.

---

## Basic load estimate

Total heat trace load is estimated as:

$$ P_{total} = L \cdot q $$

**Where**

- $L$ = total length (m)
- $q$ = heat output (W/m)

Circuit current estimate:

$$ I = P_{total} / V $$
